I don't want to stop being online. I just want the volume lower.

Private Instagram, public site. A newsletter that arrives in your inbox instead of interrupting your feed. A comments section you have to sign in to use. None of this is hiding. It's just moving the party to a room that fits the guests.

## The side door

The internet used to have side doors. Personal sites, mailing lists, forums that required you to know a URL. The front door got very big and very loud, and it turns out a lot of the actual conversation was happening in the quieter rooms anyway.

I'm rebuilding my side door here. Come in if you want.
